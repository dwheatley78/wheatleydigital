Introducing myself


Hi my name is David, I've spent over 15 years driving organisational change across operational, logistical and engineering business functions, in companies both large and small. I have always been fascinated by computers, as a child I learnt to programme writing simple games on an old Commodore, a passion I know inflict on other kids though our Kids Code Club.

In more recent years I have had the opportunity to combine my interests in manufacturing and software, running the application development and support team at a major international aerospace company. Within this role I have been trained, and instrumental in implementing, design thinking, scrum project management and eco-system mapping all of which have been hugely valuable in helping to identify opportunities and implement cultural change across the organisation.

In 2021 I completed an MSc in business data analytics, which has helped to consolidate my knowledge of implementing statistical, machine learning , simulation and decision modelling tools to deliver business value. This unique combination of change management, software development and advanced analytical skills enable me to offer a unique value proposition, which I'm confident can support you on your digital service transformation journey.

The EMPEDATA difference


Driving a digital service transformation programme is inevitably a voyage into the unknown, but it doesn't need to be intimidating. At EMPEDATA we specialise in developing supportable and affordable solutions, perfect for SME organisations that recognise the benefits of becoming more digital. As your partner we work alongside you to identify opportunities and build solutions that will enhance your decision-making capabilities, increase your operational efficiency and help to deliver new digital business models

On the other pages of this site we outline the 3 core elements of our digital service transformation programmes and the range of support we provide:

  • Strategise - Our "Design Thinking" workshop facilitation is the perfect way to define your strategic objectives and drive innovation, while our delivery team can develop the solutions and coach members of your team to help you grow an enduring digital capability.
  • Analyse - Our 4 week demonstrator package delivers value fast, providing actionable insights to drive increased efficiency and effectivity into your business.
  • Productionise - We help you develop a robust data platform to reliably capture, store, process and present the required information to provide long-term sustainable capability.

Once you have started on your digital service transformation journey we can then work with you to tackle the 4 main challenges to continue and grow your capability:

  • Productionising the developed solutions
  • Scaling out to identify and address new business opportunities
  • Training and developing digital skills across your workforce
  • Embedding the cultural changes and operating procedures to fully realise the benefits digital technologies can bring.

At EMPEDATA we recognise that, even during these times of remote working, a personal touch can go a long way in helping us become a trusted part of your team. While we are happy to support businesses from across the UK our base in the beautiful village of South Petherton, Somerset allows us to provide a truly personal touch to businesses across the South West.
