Allow me to introduce myself

Hi my name is David, I've spent over 15 years creating empowered environments and driving organisational change across operational, logistical and engineering businesses functions across Europe, in companies both large and small. I have always been fascinated by computers, as a child I learnt to programme writing simple games on an old Commodore, a passion I know inflict on other kids though our Kids Code Club.

In more recent years I have had the opportunity to combine my interests in manufacturing and IT, running the application development and support team at Leonardo. Within this role I have been trained, and instrumental in implementing, design thinking, scrum project management and eco-system mapping all of which have been hugely valuable in helping to identify opportunities and implement cultural change across the organisation.

In 2021 I completed an MSc in Business data analytics, which has helped to consolidate my knowledge of implementing statistical, machine learning models, simulation and decision modelling tools to deliver business value. This unique combination of change management, software development and advanced analytical skills enable me to offer unique insights and value proposition which I'm confident can support you on your digital transformation journey. The EMPEDATA difference

Driving transformation is inevitably a voyage into the unknown, but it doesn't need to be intimidating. As your partner we work alongside you to identify opportunities where analytics can deliver real value and quickly develop prototypes to provide demonstrable capability. Our initial capability demonstration package consists of 4 one-week sprints that can serve as a catalyst for your digital transformation:

  • Week 1 - Working closely with you to scope a business opportunity, identify potential data sources and document an agreed set of success criteria.
  • Week 2 - Collating, cleaning and processing the available data into a format suitable for analysis
  • Week 3 - Develop the analytical models that provide actionable insight.
  • Week 4 - Finalise models, enhance visualisations, document and present the work performed.

Armed with evidence of the capability data analytics can provide, we work closely with you to devise a strategy to tackle four key challenges:

  • Productionising the solutions
  • Scaling out to identify and address new business opportunities
  • Training and developing digital skills across your workforce
  • Embedding the cultural changes and operating procedures to fully leverage the benefits digital technologies can bring.

At EMPEDATA we recognise that, even during these times of remote working, a personal touch can go a long way in helping us become a trusted part of your team. While we are happy to support businesses from across the UK our base in the beautiful village of South Petherton, Somerset allows us to provide a truly personal touch to businesses across the South West.
