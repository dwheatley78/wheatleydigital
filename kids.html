Making our own games using Scratch

  • Scratch is a great block based programming language, designed for kids and totally free!
  • Generally we have followed the instructions in the 25 Scratch Games for Kids book
  • Check out some of our work using the links below

FOOTBALL

Aim: This is a 2 player game and the aim is to be the first to score 5 goals

Controls: Blue team use 'A' to move up and 'Z' to move down. Red Team use up and down cursor keys

ATTACK OF THE DONUT

Aim: Shoot the donuts before they hit you ship

Controls: Use the left and right cursor keys to spin the ship and space bar to shoot.

SPIN CATCH

Aim: Spin your ship to catch the balls with the matching colour

Controls: Use the left and right cursor keys to spin the ship

SNAKE

Aim: See how long you can make the snake without touching the walls or your tail

Controls: Use the left and right cursor keys to turn the snake

MONSTER INVASION

Aim: Shoot the beasts before they get you!

Controls: Use the left and right cursor keys to move the space ship and space bar to shoot

PATTERN MAKER

Aim: See how interesting a pattern you can make

Controls: Use the left and right cursor keys to spin and up to move.
