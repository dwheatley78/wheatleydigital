Aircraft Prognostics


With the growing development of autonomous air vehicles we were commissioned to develop a prototype solution to monitor the health of theaircraft and optimise the associated maintenance activities to drive down coat and maximise availability.



The developed solution utilised an LSTM deep learning model to capture the temporal nature of the aircraft sensor data normalised by the operating conditions to minimise the impact of the operating conditions.

The results of the remaining useful life calculations combined with a simulated physical degradation were then used to populate a Reduced Order Model (ROM) digital twin and supporting optimisation algorithm to determine to optimum maintenance actions dependent on a range of objectives and capacity and material availability constraints.

A complimentary Bayesian network was used to provide probabilistic diagnostics of an observed failure.



The client is now using the proposed model as the basis of their future support service offering
